About Me

My name is Elijah Creighton,

I am a senior at the University of South Florida working towards my bachelor’s in information science with a concentration in web development technologies. With my ability and desire to learn and collaborate in rapidly changing environments and compositions, I am eager to tackle web development or design challenges to achieve lasting impacts on user experience.

I began my college career at Hillsborough Community College at the Plant City campus in August of 2018. After receiving my A.A. in computer information systems in May 2021 I decided to transfer to the University of South Florida’s Tampa campus. There I began learning the ins and outs of web development and design. I worked on a great multitude of projects during my time at USF. After creating my first website from scratch in my Web Page Design and Management class, I learned not only how to create and maintain a website, but also that I really enjoyed doing so. Creating something to solve a problem, inevitably coming across some bug that prevents it from working, then finally coming up with a fix that just works.

I was proud of what I made when I first created and published my first web site. Of course, I’d be too embarrassed to show it off now as I don’t think it’s even close to the kind of quality work, I am capable of putting out. Although I do have it saved on my hard drive somewhere. Maybe someday...
